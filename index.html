Biography

Hi! I am currently working as a Robotics Engineer at Abundant Robotics, an agricultural startup. I have graduated from the University of Maryland College Park (UMD) for Robotics Studies (UMD), where I was part of Micro Aerial Vehicle (AMAV) advised by Prof. Derek Paley. My research interests include robotics, state estimation, computer vision, and machine learning.

In my spare time, I can often be found fixing and building robots, learning guitar, watching movies, playing tennis, chess, or table tennis.
